ABOUT US

“ Human values and passion for service is our destiny”

Sathyan memorial Arts and Sports club is a best in class and one of the prestigious societies established in 1985 in the Trivandrum district of Kerala. The society is formed by Gregory, an eminent humanitarian and social worker started this with an aim of helping communities through innovative and practical approaches specifically targeting children, women and youths of the underprivileged and poorest community in the locality of the Trivandrum and has a dream of expanding this to the whole of the Kerala. Above all his involvement with the society and volume of the work done with the facilities he had is still outstanding and got accolades from the government and local bodies. The presence of the motivated and focused team with him is an added advantage which continued to inspire him and to accomplish his goals of serving people through various project models from time to time. Sathyan a famous actor from Kerala and a philanthropist given the immense thought to the founder of Sathyan memorial Arts and Sports club to offer his life for serving the public full time and that was the inception of new saga. Sathyan memorial Arts and Sports club is non-profit charity-oriented society registered under ‘Travancorecochin literary scientific and charitable societies ‘Registration Act XII of 1955 in 1985.

Achievements

The core purpose for which the Sathyan memorial arts and sports club is formed that to improve the livelihood of the poor people by closely working with them. It starts from understanding their problem at day-to-day life what hampers them to face the uncertainties in the life. Usually in the zones where below poverty line, people’s lack of awareness causes serious troubles to them. Youth remains unemployed without knowing the career opportunity exists in the market and their inability to compete in the job market when compared to others. For them, skill development programs and career guidance are required to pursue a professional course to conduct the life. Trust concentrates much on those issues.
